ALLEPPEY

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a picturesque town in Kerala, famous for its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and lush greenery. Known as the "Venice of the East," it offers tranquil canals, beautiful beaches, and vibrant local culture. Alleppey is also renowned for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race and Ayurvedic retreats.
